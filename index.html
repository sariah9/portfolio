Hi, I'm Sariah. I was an art major before I became a CS major. After 5 years of false career starts, I began taking online classes in web development.

My introduction to Javascript made me want to learn programming full-time. The idea that I could mix innovative technology with a creative mindset inspired me to pursue software engineering as a career.

When I'm not learning programming, I am a wife, a dog mom, and a world traveller. I love all creative outlets like woodworking, painting, sewing, and pottery. I eat hard and exercise harder. I also love hiking, learning new words, and doing puzzles.
